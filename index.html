Why?

The year is 2016, another font-provider-as-a-service has reluctantly shuttered. This will introduce another round of updates and amends across the network as we root out these 3rd party dependencies from our projects. We'll have to broach this topic with our clients, talk them through the whimisical nature of leased ownership and shrug this off as unfortunate. We just picked the wrong provider, happens to everyone now and again.

There are not enough reasons for us to continue following this pattern and add unnecessary dependencies into our sites. Instead let's go into business with foundries who are happy to provide us, as their customer, everything needed to self-host typefaces. It's easier to explain to our clients and easier to implement.

Missing your favourite foundry? Tweets or pull requests are welcome.
